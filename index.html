TAMIL NADU DIET
Budget-friendly local foods · ~1800–2000 kcal/day target
🎯 Goal: Eat ~500–700 kcal less than you burn. No starvation. No fancy supplements. Just real Tamil food, right portions.
⏰ 7:00 AM — Early Morning
Kickstart (Before Breakfast)
1 glass warm water + lemon OR methi seeds water (overnight soaked)
~5 kcal · Boosts metabolism, aids digestion
⏰ 8:00 AM — Breakfast
Option A: Idli + Sambar
🫓 3 medium idlis (each ~40g = 120g total) 🥣 Sambar: 1.5 katori (200ml, load with veg) 🟢 Green chutney: 2 tbsp (30g max)
~320 kcal · Protein: 10g · Low fat · Filling
⏰ 8:00 AM — Breakfast Alternative
Option B: Ragi Koozh / Ragi Kanji
🌾 Ragi flour: 4 tbsp (40g) 🥛 Buttermilk/water: 300ml 🧂 Salt to taste + 1 small shallot (optional)
~200 kcal · Excellent for weight loss · Keeps you full 4+ hours
⏰ 8:00 AM — Breakfast Alternative C
Option C: Upma / Pongal (lighter version)
🌾 Rava/Oats: 50g (⅓ cup dry) 🥬 Mixed veg: 100g (carrot, beans, peas) 🫙 Oil: max 1 tsp (5ml) while cooking 🥥 Coconut: 1 tbsp grated only
~280 kcal · Good fibre · Keep oil minimal
⏰ 11:00 AM — Mid-Morning Snack
Light Snack
🍌 1 medium banana (120g) OR 🥜 Peanuts: 30g (small handful, unsalted) OR 🥛 Buttermilk (moru): 250ml, no sugar
~100–150 kcal · Don't skip — prevents overeating at lunch
⏰ 1:00 PM — Lunch
South Indian Thali (Controlled)
🍚 Rice: 150g cooked (¾ cup) — Week 1–4 🍚 Rice: 100g cooked (½ cup) — Week 5–8 🍚 Rice: 75g cooked (⅓ cup) — Week 9–13 🥣 Sambar: 1.5 katori (200ml) 🫘 Dal/Kootu: 1 katori (150g) 🥬 Poriyal (any veg): 1 katori (100g) 🍲 Rasam: 1 small cup (100ml) 🥛 Curd/Moru: 100ml (small katori)
~500–550 kcal · Gradually reduce rice over weeks — this is the most important step
⏰ 4:00 PM — Evening Snack
Protein Snack
🥚 2 boiled eggs (100g) OR 🫘 Boiled black chana: 75g (½ cup cooked) OR 🥜 Groundnut chaat: 40g peanuts + onion + lemon
~150–200 kcal · High protein keeps cravings away till dinner
⏰ 7:30 PM — Dinner
Light Dinner (Key for fat loss!)
🫓 2 small chapati (each 30g = 60g total) OR 🌾 Kambu/Varagu millet: 100g cooked (½ cup) OR 🥬 Keerai kootu: 150g (1 large katori) 🍲 Vegetable soup: 250ml (1 full bowl) 🫘 Dal: 100ml (½ katori, for protein)
~350–400 kcal · No rice at night — this is the biggest fat-loss hack

GOLDEN RULES
Follow these and results are guaranteed
THE 10 NON-NEGOTIABLES
These make or break your journey
1
Never skip breakfast — eating in the morning kickstarts metabolism. Skipping causes overeating later.
2
No rice at night — Switch to millets, chapati, or soup at dinner. This single change can drop 3–5 kg.
3
Walk after every meal — Even 5–10 mins. Lowers blood sugar spike, improves digestion.
4
Drink water before eating — 1 glass before every meal. You'll naturally eat 15–20% less.
5
Sleep 7–8 hours — Sleep deprivation causes fat retention (cortisol hormone). Non-negotiable.
6
Don't eat while watching TV/phone — Mindless eating doubles calorie intake. Eat at the table, slowly.
7
No cool drinks / packaged juice — One Pepsi = 150 empty calories with zero nutrition. Drink moru, neer mor, lemon water instead.
8
Weigh yourself once a week only — Every Monday morning, after toilet, before eating. Daily weighing is misleading.
9
Back pain = switch exercise, not stop — If an exercise hurts your back, replace it. Don't stop the session entirely.
10
Progress photos > scale — Take photos every 2 weeks. Body recomposition doesn't always show on scale but definitely shows in mirror.

BACK PAIN TIPS
Specific to your mild back discomfort
✅
Sleep on your side with a pillow between your knees — reduces spinal pressure.
✅
Glute bridges and bird-dog exercises (in Phase 2) will significantly reduce back pain over 4–6 weeks.
✅
Losing 10 kg takes massive pressure off your lower back. Your pain will likely reduce naturally.
⚠️
Avoid sit-ups, crunches, or toe-touches. These flex the spine and can worsen back issues.
⚠️
If back pain suddenly gets severe or goes into your legs — stop exercise and see a doctor.
