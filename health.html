Principles

The rules that determine results

The 10 Non-Negotiables
1
Never skip breakfast. Skipping causes the body to conserve fat and leads to overeating by afternoon.
2
No rice at dinner. Switch to millets or chapati. This single change can cause 3–5 kg loss in month one alone.
3
Walk 10 mins after every meal. Reduces blood glucose spike by up to 30% and accelerates fat metabolism.
4
One glass water before eating. Every meal, every time. Research shows 15–20% fewer calories consumed per meal.
5
Sleep 7–8 hours. Sleep deprivation raises cortisol which directly causes fat retention — physiology, not willpower.
6
No screens while eating. Distracted eating doubles calorie intake. Eat at a table, slowly.
7
No cool drinks. Replace Pepsi and packaged juice with neer mor, moru, or lemon water permanently.
8
Weigh once a week only. Monday morning, after bathroom, before eating. Daily weighing is misleading.
9
Back pain means switch, not stop. Replace the painful exercise — do not cancel the entire session.
10
Progress photos beat the scale. Take photos every 2 weeks. Recomposition shows in the mirror before the scale.

Back Pain Protocol
Sleep on your side with a pillow between your knees — removes lumbar compression overnight.
Glute bridges and bird-dog (Phase 2) are clinically therapeutic. Expect meaningful pain reduction in 4–6 weeks.
Losing 10 kg removes ~30–40 kg of cumulative spinal compression per step. Pain likely resolves naturally.
Avoid sit-ups, crunches, and toe-touches — these flexion-load the spine and can worsen the condition.
If pain becomes acute, radiates to legs, or causes numbness — stop exercise and see a doctor immediately.
