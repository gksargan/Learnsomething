Foods to Avoid

Deep fried foods
Bajji, bonda, vada, murukku — limit to once a week maximum
Sugar and cool drinks
Tea with 2+ spoons sugar, sweets, Pepsi, Coke — zero nutritional value, high calorie
Excess white rice
Maximum 1 cup per meal. Switch to millets wherever possible.
Late night eating
Nothing solid after 21:00. Digestion slows after sunset.
Maida products
Parotta, white bread, biscuits — refined flour spikes blood sugar and causes fat storage

Principles

The rules that
determine results

The 10 Non-Negotiables
Violate one consistently and results stall
1
Never skip breakfast. Eating in the morning activates metabolism. Skipping causes the body to conserve fat and leads to overeating later.
2
No rice at dinner. Switch to millets, chapati or soup. This single change alone can result in 3–5 kg loss in the first month.
3
Walk after every meal. Even 5–10 minutes. Reduces blood glucose spike by up to 30% and meaningfully improves digestion.
4
One glass of water before eating. Every meal, every time. You will naturally consume 15–20% fewer calories.
5
Sleep 7–8 hours. Sleep deprivation elevates cortisol, which directly causes fat retention. This is physiology, not willpower.
6
No screens while eating. Distracted eating doubles calorie intake. Sit at a table, eat slowly, pay attention to the food.
7
No cool drinks or packaged juice. One 350ml Pepsi = 150 empty calories with zero nutrition. Replace with neer mor, moru, or lemon water.
8
Weigh yourself once per week only. Every Monday morning after the bathroom, before eating. Daily weighing is misleading.
9
Back pain means switch, not stop. If an exercise hurts, replace it with another. Stopping the session entirely for minor pain is counterproductive.
10
Progress photos matter more than the scale. Take a photo every two weeks. Body recomposition often does not show on the scale but is clearly visible.

Back Pain Protocol
Specific guidance for your condition
Sleep on your side with a pillow between your knees. This removes pressure from the lumbar spine.
Glute bridges and bird-dog (Phase 2) are specifically therapeutic for lower back pain and will reduce it over 4–6 weeks.
Losing 10 kg removes approximately 30–40 kg of cumulative force from your lumbar spine with every step. Your pain will likely resolve naturally.
Avoid sit-ups, crunches, and standing toe-touches. These flex-load the spine and can worsen the condition.
If back pain becomes acute, radiates into your legs, or causes numbness — stop all exercise and consult a doctor immediately.
